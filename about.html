Welcome to
Arte Pizza

Arte Pizza is proud to invite you to experience the various tastes of our authentic and traditional Italian pasta. All our pizzas are hand made to order in a traditional way with over 25 years’ experience. We use the best ingredients; fresh vegetables, quality olive oil, but most importantly: our passion. We are a family run business who take pride in slowly crafting every meal for every customer, making sure that the food and service you receive is exquisite. Our restaurant is designed to make you feel as if you are in Italy, with beautiful ambience and Italian radio in the background; on top of the sensational aroma of Italian food.
